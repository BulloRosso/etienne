An educational technology company developing innovative learning platforms and assessment tools for schools and universities.

Notable products:
* LearnSphere: Adaptive learning platform with personalized curriculum paths
* AssessMax: Comprehensive online testing and evaluation system
* ClassConnect: Virtual classroom management and collaboration tool
