# AgriTech Innovations, Iowa

A precision agriculture company developing smart farming solutions and crop monitoring technologies.

Notable products:
* CropMaster: Satellite-based crop monitoring and yield prediction system
* SmartIrrigation: IoT-enabled precision watering and nutrient delivery platform
* FarmAnalytics: Comprehensive farm management and financial planning software
