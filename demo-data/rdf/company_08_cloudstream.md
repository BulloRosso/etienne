A cloud computing company specializing in media streaming and content delivery network technologies.

Notable products:
* StreamMax: High-definition video streaming platform with adaptive bitrate technology
* ContentAccelerator: Global content delivery network with edge computing capabilities
* MediaVault: Secure cloud storage and digital rights management system
