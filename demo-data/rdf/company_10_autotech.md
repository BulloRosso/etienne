# AutoTech Dynamics, Michigan

An automotive technology company specializing in autonomous vehicle systems and smart transportation solutions.

Notable products:
* DriveAssist: Advanced driver assistance system with machine learning capabilities
* FleetOptimizer: Intelligent fleet management and route optimization software
* CarConnect: Vehicle-to-everything (V2X) communication platform
