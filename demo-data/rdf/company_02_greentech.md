# GreenTech Innovations, Oregon

A sustainable technology company focused on renewable energy solutions and smart grid technologies.

Notable products:
* SolarOptimizer: Smart solar panel management system with predictive maintenance
* EcoGrid Manager: Intelligent power distribution software for renewable energy networks
* WindTracker: Advanced wind turbine monitoring and efficiency optimization platform
