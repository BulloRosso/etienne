## Product Line Overview

EuroBatt manufactures lithium-ion battery cells in three chemistry families across multiple form factors. All cells are produced at our Nürnberg facility and comply with EU Battery Regulation 2023/1542.

## Product Matching Guide

When matching a customer request to a product:

1. **Chemistry is mandatory.** NMC and LFP are not interchangeable for a given request. If the customer specifies NMC, only match NMC products. If they specify "lithium-ion" without a chemistry preference, consider both families and note the trade-offs.

2. **Form factor is mandatory.** Cylindrical, prismatic, and pouch cells have different mechanical integration requirements. Never substitute one for another.

3. **Capacity matching.** Match within ±10% of the customer's requested capacity. If the customer's request falls between two products, match the closer one and note the delta.

4. **High-density options.** If the customer's capacity request exceeds the standard value but is within the high-density option, configure accordingly. High-density carries a surcharge (see feature-description.md).

5. **Discharge rate.** If the customer needs a higher discharge rate than the standard product offers, check if a High Power variant exists (e.g., BC-NMC-2170-HP). Flag the trade-off in capacity and cycle life.

6. **Multiple matches.** If multiple products could satisfy the request, rank by: (a) closest capacity match, (b) best cycle life match, (c) standard over custom configuration.
