- Rated flow: 700 GPD (≈ 2.65 m³/day) at standard test conditions.
- Stabilised salt rejection: 99.4 %.

- Boron rejection: ~88 %.
- Max operating pressure: 1 000 psi (69 bar).

- Free-chlorine tolerance: < 0.1 ppm.
- Recommended SDI feed: < 5, preferably < 3.
